What is AR-js Studio?

AR.js Studio is an open source project with the goal of remove the barrier of coding knowledge so everyone can easily create, deploy, and share AR projects with the world.

Last june we launched the first version of the platform, addressing the need for accessible AR tools for educators and creative communities, and now we are ready for next step.
