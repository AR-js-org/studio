Marker-based

A marker-based AR experience uses an image to anchor the AR content. In order to create it, you will first upload a custom image, to generate your marker, and then upload the content you want to show when marker is recognised by the camera.

Once you have created it, you will obtain a marker image that you have to give to the users, either on print paper or shown on screen.
