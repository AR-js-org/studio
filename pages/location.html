Location-based

A location-based AR experience uses GPS coordinates to place your AR content in a specific location. You can read more here, or follow the steps below to get started now.

1. Pick the location

Click to drop a pin on the map below or enter exact GPS coordinates.

2. Choose the content

Select the type of content you would like to appear and then upload the file from your computer. Your uploaded content will appear in all the locations you have set above.
